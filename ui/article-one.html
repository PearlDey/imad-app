dream 1

She woke up to music. A sweet lullaby. Swaying to its tune she danced outside. She saw seven rainbows up in the glittering black sky. The source of the music was a bird up high. She chirped back to it as if to say "hii".

She walked down to the river, where mermaids were chatting. She looked around to see how fireflies had lit up the sweet dark night. With colourful blue, red, purple and several other lights.
